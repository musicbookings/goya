About

Welcome to the musical world of Goran (Goya) Gojevic—where passion meets performance. Goran is a seasoned clarinetist and saxophonist renowned for his versatility and captivating performances. With nearly 40 years of experience under his belt, he brings a unique blend of artistry and professionalism to every event.

Goran specializes in providing unforgettable music for:

  • Corporate Events
  • Private Parties
  • Restaurants & Entertainment Venues
  • Weddings & Special Occasions

Gallery

Why Choose Goya

Goran’s extensive repertoires on clarinet and saxophone span across various genres, including classical, jazz, pop, latin, and more. He tailors his performances to match the theme and mood of your event, ensuring a personalized experience. Goran is a solo musician who plays with accompaniment tracks; however he works with several live ensembles and bands as needed.
